# Advisory & Consulting

We help founder-led teams make better product, AI, and operational decisions — and build the software when it's the right move.

Work is structured. Outcomes are defined. Accountability is explicit. We bring the same judgment we develop building our own [ventures](/venture-studio/): what matters now, what can wait, and how to sequence decisions correctly.

---

## How We Help

We work with founders and executives at three levels, depending on where you are and what you need.

**Build** — You have an idea and want it to become real software. We embed as a product partner: shaping the concept, making technical decisions, and driving execution. You stay focused on the business; we make sure the right thing gets built and shipped. This is the fastest path from vision to something in market.

**Advise** — You already have a team building. We come in as a fractional CPO or product lead: setting direction with OKRs, making roadmap decisions, and keeping execution connected to strategy. Useful when the engineering is there but product leadership isn't.

**Accelerate** — You want to move faster and spend less building what you already know needs to exist. We bring AI-native development practices into your process — not as a novelty, but as genuine leverage on the time and cost of building, testing, and iterating.

---

## Situations We Work With

**You have a real idea and it isn't moving.**
The concept is clear. The business case exists. But the gap between "idea" and "working software" keeps widening. We bridge that gap — not by writing requirements documents, but by making decisions and shipping things.

**You have a team but no product direction.**
Engineers are building. Stakeholders have opinions. Nothing is connecting. We come in as product lead: setting the right objectives, cutting scope, and keeping execution pointed at what actually matters.

**You're scaling the wrong thing.**
Revenue exists. The product works. But now there's pressure to grow, and it's not clear what to build next. The biggest risk isn't technical failure — it's building the wrong abstraction. We help sequence correctly before you invest at scale.

**You want AI in your product but don't know where.**
AI is not a feature. It's a capability that changes what's possible in your product. We help identify where AI creates real leverage — and where it's theater.

## How an Engagement Starts

Most engagements begin with a conversation about what you're trying to build and why. From there, the shape of the work becomes clear quickly — sometimes a short sprint to validate an idea, sometimes an ongoing partnership.

We don't take on many projects at once. When we're in, we're genuinely in.

---

## Who This Works Best For

Founders and executives with a real idea and real stakes. People who value judgment over activity, and who want a partner who can both think through the hard questions and make things real.

Engagements work best when there's clear ownership, honest feedback, and space to learn from what gets built.
