# Consultancy

The Consultancy works with external teams to build real products.

Work is structured. Outcomes are defined. Accountability is explicit.

The Consultancy applies the same thinking developed inside the Practice — judgment about what matters now, what can wait, and how to sequence decisions correctly.

---

## How I Can Help

I work with founders and executives at three levels, depending on where you are and what you need.

**Build** — You have an idea and want it to become real software. I embed as a product partner: shaping the concept, making technical decisions, and driving execution. You stay focused on the business; I make sure the right thing gets built and shipped. This is the fastest path from vision to something in market.

**Advise** — You already have a team building. I come in as a fractional CPO or product lead: setting direction with OKRs, making roadmap decisions, and keeping execution connected to strategy. Useful when the engineering is there but product leadership isn't.

**Accelerate** — You want to move faster and spend less building what you already know needs to exist. I bring AI-native development practices into your process — not as a novelty, but as genuine leverage on the time and cost of building, testing, and iterating.

---

## Situations I Work With

**You have a real idea and it isn't moving.**
The concept is clear. The business case exists. But the gap between "idea" and "working software" keeps widening. I bridge that gap — not by writing requirements documents, but by making decisions and shipping things.

**You have a team but no product direction.**
Engineers are building. Stakeholders have opinions. Nothing is connecting. I come in as product lead: setting the right objectives, cutting scope, and keeping execution pointed at what actually matters.

**You're scaling the wrong thing.**
Revenue exists. The product works. But now there's pressure to grow, and it's not clear what to build next. The biggest risk isn't technical failure — it's building the wrong abstraction. I help sequence correctly before you invest at scale.

**You want AI in your product but don't know where.**
AI is not a feature. It's a capability that changes what's possible in your product. I help identify where AI creates real leverage — and where it's theater.

---

## How an Engagement Starts

Most engagements begin with a conversation about what you're trying to build and why. From there, the shape of the work becomes clear quickly — sometimes a short sprint to validate an idea, sometimes an ongoing partnership.

I don't take on many projects at once. When I'm in, I'm genuinely in.

---

## Who This Works Best For

Founders and executives with a real idea and real stakes. People who value judgment over activity, and who want a partner who can both think through the hard questions and make things real.

Engagements work best when there's clear ownership, honest feedback, and space to learn from what gets built.

The Consultancy is not a staffing pool for the Practice.
